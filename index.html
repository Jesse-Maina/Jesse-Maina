About Me

I have spent over a decade building, fixing and securing networks — from small business offices in Nairobi to enterprise multi-site WANs spanning several locations. Along the way I have configured everything from Cisco routers and VLANs to IP-PBX systems and enterprise wireless networks.

Since relocating to the UK I have kept building: I am now studying Cloud Computing at Arden University, working toward my AWS and Azure certifications, and sharpening my skills in Python and AI-driven tools.

I am someone who learns fast, works well independently, and takes real pride in solving problems properly. I thrive in remote environments — I am self-organised, communicative, and comfortable managing my own workload.
